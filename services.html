Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, I offer monthly retainer packages that cover security updates, performance monitoring, regular backups, and dedicated hours for new feature implementations.

How long does a typical custom web application take to build?

A standard custom web application usually takes between 8 to 12 weeks from discovery to launch, depending on the complexity of features and integrations required.

Do you work with existing codebases or only start from scratch?

I frequently take over existing projects. Before beginning any active development, I typically perform a comprehensive code audit to ensure the existing architecture is stable, secure, and ready to scale.

What is your typical payment structure?

For custom development projects, payments are generally divided into milestones: an initial deposit to secure the schedule, followed by payments tied to specific deliverables, and a final payment upon successful deployment.
